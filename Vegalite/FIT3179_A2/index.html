World Income, Education and Health

World Economics often contains useful information for data analysis. Income
and GDP are the most interesting topics of all
time. In 1978, the first World Development Report introduced groupings
of low income and middle income countries. In the 1983
WDR, the Middle Income Group was split into lower middle
and upper middle groups, and in 1989 a high income
country definition was introduced.

Human Development Index

Now we know that World Income has been increasing as green colour increases, we can see how the world has developed through years. We use a measure called the Human Development Index. The HDI is a summary composite measure of a country's average achievements in three basic aspects of human development: Health, Knowledge and Standard of living.

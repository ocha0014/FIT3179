World Income, Education and Health

World Economics useful information for data analysis. Income is one of the most interesting topic of all time. In 1978, the first World Development Report introduced groupings of "low income" and "middle income" countries. In the 1983 WDR, the "middle income group" was split into "lower middle" and "upper middle"groups. and in 1989 a "high income" country definition was introduced.

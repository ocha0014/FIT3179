World Income, Education and Health

World Economics often contains useful information for data analysis. Income
and GDP are the most interesting topics of all
time. In 1978, the first World Development Report introduced groupings
of low income and middle income countries. In the 1983
WDR, the Middle Income Group was split into lower middle
and upper middlegroups, and in 1989 a "high income"
country definition was introduced.
